# Priority System Update ✅

## Simple Priority Rule

**Duration > 6 months = High Priority**
**Duration = 6 months = Normal Priority**

## Duration Options
- 6 Months → **Normal Priority** (Blue)
- 12 Months → **High Priority** (Orange)
- 18 Months → **High Priority** (Orange)
- 24 Months → **High Priority** (Orange)

## Why This Works

Long-term projects (>6 months) need components for extended periods, so they get higher priority for approval and resource allocation.

Short-term projects (6 months) are standard requests with normal priority.

## Visual Indicators

- **High Priority**: Orange badge
- **Normal Priority**: Blue badge

The priority is automatically determined by the selected duration - no complex calculations needed!
